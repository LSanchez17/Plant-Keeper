Welcome to Plant Keeper!

What is Plant Keeper?

Plant keeper is an application to help you keep track of your plants! Keep track of when to trim, repot, and water your plants. Accomodates for both indoor and outdoor plants, along with a weather forecast for any outdoor plant needs. Add new plants to your account anytime, group them by name or location, and search for plants through an external API, and even add those to your account!
